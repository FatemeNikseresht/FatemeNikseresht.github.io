I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Virginia. I work under the supervision of Dr. Bradford Campbell. My research focuses on human behavior modeling using data streams collected through wearables and embedded systems.

I received my Bachelor's degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Shiraz University advised by Dr. Alireza Keshavarz-Hadad.
